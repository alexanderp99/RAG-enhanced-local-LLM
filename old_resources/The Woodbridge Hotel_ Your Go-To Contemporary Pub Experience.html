Welcome to The Woody

Housed inside a gorgeous federation style building, The Woodbridge Hotel looks quaint on the outside, but on the inside beats the heart of a fresh contemporary pub bistro. With a great menu, rockin’ local bands, fun trivia nights, The Woodbridge is more than meets the eye.

Whether you want to kick up your heels and be entertained, relax and dine out with friends and family or bring your pooch pals along for some fun in the beer garden, pop in to the Woodbridge Hotel where there’s something happening every night of the week!

Eat & Drink

If you’re hungry or just a bit peckish, The Woody has an extensive menu that caters for all tastes. There are tasting plates, delicious pastas, soups, sandwiches, burgers, kids menu options and much more! All food is prepared fresh by the Woodbridge chefs from local suppliers.

Private Events & Functions

Looking for the perfect venue to host your upcoming event? Look no further than The Woody.

We have several unique and versatile function spaces that will suit any occasion, whether it’s a corporate gathering, a milestone celebration, a wedding reception, or a casual get-together with friends and family.
